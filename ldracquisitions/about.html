Who Am I?

I am a student worker

In that case, all you need to do is go the front page, select the type of acquisition (is this all digital or are there parts of this acquisition that are physical). From there, you must fill out the form completely and be sure and add all the required information in the sidebar. Once you have filled out the form completey, you sould click on the "Save" button. If you have filled out the form correctly, you will be directed to a confirmation page. If you have not filled out the form correctly, you will see the problems displayed at the top of the page. You should correct all of the errors listed and try clicking "Save" again when you think you have corrected the mistakes.

I am a Digital Accessions Specialist

Bad news: you've got a little bit more to do than the student worker. Good news: it's not that much extra work.

You need to make sure the acquisition form that the student worker filled out is correct. You also need to add all extra required information for an accession record before submitting it for final inclusion in a stage directory.

You should click on the list acquisitions navigation link at the top of the page, and select the acquisiton record that you want to accession. Once you have selected that record, you should correct any part of the form that needs to be corrected and fill out the extra information. When you have completed the form to your satisfaction, click the "Save" button. If there are mistakes, you will see a list of errors at the top of the page. You should correct those errors and click "Save" again. If there are no mistakes, you will be directed to a download link for the accession record.
